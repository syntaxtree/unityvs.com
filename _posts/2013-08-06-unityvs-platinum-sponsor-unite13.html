--- layout: post title: UnityVS is a platinum sponsor of Unite 2013 date: 2013-08-06 16:00:00 ---

UnityVS, your plugin to write and debug your Unity games inside Visual Studio finishes its Unite conference tour in a bang!

The UnityVS team is super proud to be a Platinum Sponsor of Unite 2013 in Vancouver, the biggest Unity conference of the year.

We'll have a splendid booth with UnityVS licenses to win every day, we'll be doing a sponsored session and we'll have some pretty nice things to announce! Keep an eye on the Unite 2013 schedule.

This event is especially important for the UnityVS team as it marks the one year anniversary of the initial release of UnityVS. The last year has been a fantastic journey: UnityVS is now used by thousands of teams all over the world, from indies working at night on their games, to AAA studios.

We're looking forward to seeing you at the UnityVS booth and celebrating with you!
