After Seattle, our next stop on our Unity Meetup Tour is in San Francisco

On the evening of January the 30th, we'll be making a UnityVS presentation at the Bay Areay Unity User Group. As it is now traditional, attendees will have the chance to win a couple UnityVS Personal license, so make sure to join!

What we wrote for the previous meetup is still very much true:

Not only will we be talking about UnityVS, but we'll also talk about what was is it like to write a tool targeting Unity, and the Unity ecosystem itself. And after the talk of course, there will be plenty of room for discussions. We're hoping to meet you there!

As usual, please feel free to contact us if you would like a UnityVS presentation at your local user group.
